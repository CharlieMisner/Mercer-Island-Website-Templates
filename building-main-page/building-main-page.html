About The Building Team:

The building team creates and maintains a safe and pleasant physical environment on Mercer Island by: ensuring compliance with codes; providing a public information and permit counter; review of new construction; and issuing building permits and inspections.

The staff is committed to providing exceptional customer service.  This team is trained in both code administration and permitting processes and is available to assist citizens or business owners with questions or concerns throughout the permit process.
